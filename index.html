Privacy Policy

AI ShotMind – Smart Screenshot Cleaner

Developer: YuKiMind Studio

Package: com.shotmind.ai.snapclean

Effective Date: 2025

1. Overview

AI ShotMind is designed with privacy as a core principle. All image processing and AI classification is performed entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your photos or personal data on any external server.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not upload your screenshots or photos to any server
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or account details
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not require you to create an account

3. How the App Works

AI ShotMind requests access to your device's photo library solely to:

  • Read and analyze screenshots stored on your device
  • Display thumbnails within the app interface
  • Delete screenshots at your explicit request via the system API

All AI processing — including OCR (text recognition) and image classification — runs locally on your device using on-device machine learning models (Google ML Kit). No image data is sent to external servers at any point.

4. Data Stored Locally

AI ShotMind stores the following data locally on your device only:

DataPurpose
Screenshot metadata (asset ID, file path, classification result)To display scan results and remember processed items
Saved screenshot records (asset ID only)To filter previously saved screenshots from future scans

This data is stored in a local database on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Permissions Requested

PermissionReason
READ_MEDIA_IMAGESRead screenshots for analysis
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGERequired for Android 8–12 compatibility
ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATIONAccess full image metadata
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGERequired to delete photos via system API on older Android versions

We only request permissions necessary for core functionality. You can revoke permissions at any time in your device settings. Revoking photo access will prevent the app from functioning.

6. Third-Party Services

AI ShotMind uses the following on-device SDKs:

  • Google ML Kit (Text Recognition & Image Labeling) — runs entirely on-device, no data is sent to Google through this integration.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects anonymous crash reports to help us improve stability. This data does not include photos or personal information. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

AI ShotMind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
